About Me

My name is Susanna Matoyan, I am 21 years old. I love writing code because, for me, it’s not just a skill or a job—it’s a way to fully express myself. Even when creating a single piece of logic, there are countless paths to take, and I have the freedom to choose my own unique, individual path to solve each problem. I pour not only my knowledge and skills into it but also my inner soul, imagination, and creativity—everything that makes the process alive and one-of-a-kind. For me, coding is like painting is for an artist: if they use colors, I use logic, algorithms, and functions. Every line of code is an opportunity to create something unique, to experiment, and to give free rein to my thoughts. This process is so absorbing that I can completely immerse myself in it, forgetting about time, meals, and everything else, fully losing myself in a world of ideas and possibilities. Code is my space for freedom, creativity, and self-expression, where every problem becomes a chance to showcase my individuality and put the fullest part of myself into the solution.
