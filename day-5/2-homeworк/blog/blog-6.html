Dreams & Goals

Dreams and goals help me move forward. They give motivation and help me
find the strength to fight. Sometimes it is very hard to move toward
what you want. You may have to walk along a very thin line, sometimes
falling off and needing to climb the mountain again. You can return to
the beginning many times, but in reality you never truly return to the
start. After each fall, it takes less time to rise and move on. This
means there is progress, and if there is progress, you are already far
from the starting point.

Never Give Up

The main thing is not to give up — to strive and work hard. I believe
that any effort is always appreciated. Any hard work will definitely
bear fruit. It will not go unnoticed. The main thing is never to tell
yourself “I can’t”. If you have a goal, you can do anything, as long
as you give your maximum and support yourself.

Every step, even the smallest, brings you closer to your dreams and
goals.
